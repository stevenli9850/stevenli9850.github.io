About Myself

March 3, 2022 in Resume, Personal Introduction

My Name is Jiahui Li who currently attempt in BSIS Major in iSchool. My interst in Artificial Intelligence, For my future career life. I wish I could continue to explore with this field.

With the development of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and data science, a certain number of fields has utilised AI as a labour force under the dangerous scenarios, e.g. care of patients infected with the coronavirus. This kind of progress is very important to protect human safety and can be potentially developed to a wider range of areas. The successful applications using AI is based on decades of unremitting exploration and accumulation. When I was a little boy, the AI products were very attractive to me. Before the national college entrance examination, I chose computer science as my major with no hesitation. During this one-year study, I realise that my interest is exactly the programming and data science, especially the data safety. It can provide me with a strong sense of achievement by figuring out the vulnerabilities in a program for the establishment of a security network. With strong enthusiasm in this area, I have obtained satisfied GPA of 3.82, with several As in computer science and maths modules. Importantly, I also achieved various of awards, including the winner of National honour society for 3 consecutive years since 2017, Certificate of academic excellence in intermediate English international learning, Excellent work through the semester and Math honour society, respectively.

Between the year of 2016 and 2020, I have been a volunteer at victory Christian church & Agape Chinese baptism church who was main responsible for the PowerPoint making and playing. During the 4 years volunteering there, different topics were held by us weekly. It trained me with a strong logical thinking and communication skills. A lot of inspirational stories and experience were also delivered by my friends. It encouraged me to make most use of my time and spare no effort to pursue what I truly love in my life. Consequently, I further deepened my determination to transfer this year to have a better education with more cutting-edge and practical knowledge as well as the advanced equipment for researches.
